Sudoku

To enter a digit in a cell:

  • Touch the cell (becomes blue), then touch the digit in the bottom row
Or
  • Touch the digit in the bottom row (becomes blue), then touch the cell
To delete a cell, use the "X" button instead of a digit.
To cancel a touch, touch twice.

After input:
  • The edited cell is protected from the solver (becomes orange)
  • The unprotected cells are deleted
  • The solver is reset
To solve the sudoku, press the "SOLVE" button.

Icon Function
