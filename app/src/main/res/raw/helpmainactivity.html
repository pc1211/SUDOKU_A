Sudoku

The cells modified by the user are protected from the solver (orange color).
However, a distinct protection can be provided by pressing the "> PROTECT Distinct" button.
The color of the protected cells will then change from orange to blue.

To modify a cell, press its button:

  • In case of blank input, the cell is deleted
  • After input:
    • The edited cell is protected from the solver
    • The non protected cells are deleted
    • The solver is reset
To solve the sudoku, press the "SOLVE" button.
  • The "SOLVE" button becomes green if a solution is found, or red if impossible to solve
  • Press it again to display the next solution
