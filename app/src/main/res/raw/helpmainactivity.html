Sudoku

To enter a cell value, press its button:

  • In case of blank input, the cell is deleted
  • After input of a digit:
    • The cell is protected against the solver
    • The non protected cells are deleted
    • The solver is reset
To protect permanently the already protected cells, press the "> PERMANENT" button.
The permanently protected cells could represent a sudoku as published in a newspaper.

To solve the sudoku, press the "SOLVE" button.
  • The "SOLVE" button becomes green if a solution is found, or red if impossible to solve
  • Press it again to display the next solution
